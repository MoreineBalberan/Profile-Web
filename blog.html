Breaking: iKON’s Bobby Announces Marriage And Fiancée’s Pregnancy

iKON’s Bobby has shared big news! On August 20, Bobby took to his Instagram with a handwritten letter for fans. The letter reads as follows: Hello, this is iKON’s Bobby. There is something I really want to tell you today, so I am writing this after putting in much thought. I have promised marriage with a person I love. I am also becoming a father in September. I am happy to welcome a new family member, but I feel more apologetic to the fans who must be taken aback by my news. I should’ve let you know earlier, but I apologize that I am letting you know later as I was worried more than anything for this reason. I feel heavy responsibility as I think I have caused a great emotional burden to the people who always help and support me who is lacking a lot in many ways. I sincerely apologize to the people who are hurt or confused by my news. I will become a person who does not cause shame to my parents along with the iKON members and fans who have made my lacking self into someone who is needed in this world. More than anything, I will work even harder to not cause harm to the members and the fans who are waiting for iKON’s activities.

iKON’s Bobby And Fiancée Welcome The Birth Of Their Child

iKON’s Bobby and his fiancée welcomed their first child to the world! A source from YG Entertainment revealed on September 27, “Bobby informed us that he recently welcomed a baby boy.” Last month, Bobby personally announced through a handwritten letter that he was engaged and would become a father in September. Congratulations to Bobby and his significant other!
